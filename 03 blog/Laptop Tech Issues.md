# Laptop Tech Issues

My laptop's been having an issue and I'm not quite sure of the reason behind. I have it solved twice and want to keep the answer handy so here it goes. ## The Issue(?) When I turn on my laptop and the F11 key (which controls Airplane mode) stays orange, my laptop is unable to turn on the wifi, rendering it pretty useless. ## The Solutions So Far 1. Turn off laptop. Turn on and press ESC multiple times until bringing up the Menu > Keyboard Test > Go through the test > Boot laptop. 2. Turn off laptop. Turn on and press ESC multiple times until bringing up the Menu > BIOS Menu > Update BIOS > Wait for updates to be installed > Wait for the laptop to boot up. Backing up the files in my laptop has gone up in my list of priorities.
