Hullo! My name is Mary!

This is my first neocities site. I want to make this a space to share my creative hobbies like crochet, creative writing, and web development.

I decided to join neocities after cohost (an independent social media platform) shut down. I have been working on a personal website and while I didn't use cohost as must as I wanted to, I yielded to the largest number of users who reccomended Neocities and I'm starting the project from scratch with a less professional style (maybe).

I'm inspired by the creativity, personal narratives, and passions of the indie web. I recently finished watching Jellyfish Can't Swim in the Night and was attracted to the idea of online identities as a form for self-expression independent of online marketability.
