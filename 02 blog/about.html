About me

Hello, I'm Mary (they/them)! I'm a fannish writer in my late twenties who has been somewhat active on fandom spaces for about a decade.

This journal hosts a copy of most of my posts from previous social media (mostly Tumblr) accounts. Currently, I post about meta, media roundups, recs, and the occasional experimental post. My journal is strictly about my fannish interests so it's highly unlikely you'll see anything about my personal irl life outside of my hobbies. It's also fairly new so please bear with me as I'm still learning how this site works.

My fannish hobbies are writing fics, developing OCs, and psychoanalyzing fictional characters. Reading manga, playing videogames, watching series. Web development, web design, and web accessibility. I maintain my own websites.
