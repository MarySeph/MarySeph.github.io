## Materials
- Hook: 4.5mm
- Yarn: Cttn, Scrubby cttn; Color: White & Lilac.
- Scissors
- Yarn needle
- Opt: Stitch markers

## Methods

> [! IMPORTANT]
> Double up the yarn.

### Body 
- Square of a certain width.
### Border
- 3 sc in corners; sc on each stitch.
